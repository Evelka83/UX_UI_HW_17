WRITING & OTHER WORK

I am a research scientist, psychologist, and UX/UI designer. My research interests involve a hybrid of psychology, UX/UI design, public health, multimedia, and innovation. Much of my work has focused on examining factors that predict the engagement in healthy behaviors. I am particularly interested in the role of multidisciplinary fields, design, UX/UI design, arts & music, and innovation in effective communication and behavior change.

As a self-proclaimed life-long learner, I am always on the lookout for knowledge, discovery, and enriching experiences. Inspired by aesthetics, clarity, and the beauty of human experience, I use my background in psychology, UX/UI design, and science, to create immersive and enriching educational experiences.

I am currently an Associate Research Scientist at Columbia University Irving Medical Center.
